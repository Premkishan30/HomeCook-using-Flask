ABOUT ME!

I am Prem, a passionate foodie and culinary enthusiast who has created this recipe website to help students who are new to cooking. With a deep love for cooking and a desire to share that passion with others, our founder understands that many students struggle with cooking due to their busy schedules and lack of experience in the kitchen. That's why this website was created - to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Our founder has carefully curated a wide variety of easy-to-follow recipes that are perfect for beginners and seasoned cooks alike. With a focus on using simple ingredients and step-by-step instructions, our founder's goal is to inspire and empower students to create delicious, healthy meals from scratch.
